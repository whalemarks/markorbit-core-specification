# Purpose

This document defines the canonical business entities recognised by Mo Business.

Business Entities represent observable business objects whose commercial significance can be evaluated.

They are not persistence entities.

They are not implementation classes.

They are architecture-level business concepts.

Every Business Entity originates from factual information stored in Mo Data.

Mo Business never creates entities.

Mo Business evaluates them.

---

# Design Principle

Mo Business evaluates entities.

It never invents entities.

Every entity must already exist as a factual object.

Business Evaluation always begins with observable evidence.

Commercial Demand

Marketplace Popularity

# Entity Independence

Every Business Entity remains independent.

Entities may relate to one another.

However, Business Evaluation never changes entity identity.

Relationships belong to Mo Data.

Evaluation belongs to Mo Business.

---

# Observable Evidence

Business Evaluation requires observable evidence.

Examples include:

Views

Clicks

Collections

Enquiries

Orders

Revenue

Response Time

Completion Rate

Interaction

Historical Records

Observable evidence is the foundation of every Business Evaluation.

---

# Entity Lifecycle

Business Entities originate in Mo Data.

Mo Business evaluates them.

Mo Intelligence reasons about them.

Capability executes actions upon them.

Workspace presents them.

Applications deliver them.

Mo Business therefore occupies one stage of the architecture lifecycle.

---

# Future Expansion

Future Core versions may introduce additional Business Entities.

Examples:

Campaign

Task

Project

Brand

Channel

Every new entity shall satisfy two conditions.

It must:

exist as factual information,

be observable through business evidence.

Otherwise it does not belong to Mo Business.

---

# Summary

Business Entities define what Mo Business evaluates.

They are observable business objects originating from factual information.

Mo Business never owns the entities themselves.

It owns only their Business Evaluation.
