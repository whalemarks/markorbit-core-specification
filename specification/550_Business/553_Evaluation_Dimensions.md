# Purpose

This document defines the canonical Business Views produced by Mo Business.

A Business View represents the business interpretation of an observable fact.

Business Views do not replace factual entities.

Business Views provide an additional interpretation dimension.

---

# Design Principle

Facts remain factual.

Views remain interpretive.

Business Views shall never become facts.

Business Views shall never replace professional understanding.

---

# Canonical Business Views

The initial Business Layer defines the following Business Views.

Business Value

Business Opportunity

Business Health

Business Growth

Business Contribution

Business Performance

Business ROI

Business Score

Business Ranking

Business Trend

Each Business View evaluates one business dimension.

---

# Business Value View

Represents the overall commercial significance of an entity.

Typical evaluation includes:

commercial demand

market attractiveness

potential contribution

business importance

---

# Business Opportunity View

Represents observable opportunity.

Evaluation examples:

lead quality

market demand

customer readiness

sales opportunity

---

# Business Health View

Measures the current operational condition.

Examples:

portfolio health

customer activity

marketplace vitality

---

# Business Growth View

Represents measurable growth.

Examples:

traffic growth

order growth

content growth

revenue growth

---

# Business Contribution View

Represents measurable contribution.

Examples:

generated enquiries

generated orders

generated revenue

generated traffic

---

# Business ROI View

Measures investment efficiency.

Examples:

content ROI

campaign ROI

workflow ROI

automation ROI

---

# Business Score View

Normalised evaluation.

Allows comparison between entities.

---

# Relationship

Business Views consume factual information.

Business Views remain independent from Professional Views.

Both become inputs for Intelligent Reasoning.

---

# Summary

Business Views define how Mo Business interprets observable business evidence.

They extend factual information with measurable business significance while preserving the independence of every architecture layer.
