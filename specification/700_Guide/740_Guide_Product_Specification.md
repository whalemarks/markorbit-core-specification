# Purpose

Professional services create value only when they become complete professional experiences.

The purpose of Guide Product is to publish reusable, understandable and trustworthy professional experiences.

Guide Product answers one question.

> **What professional experience has been delivered?**

---

# Guide Product Philosophy

Capability Products deliver professional services.

Guide Products deliver professional experiences.

Guide Products represent the published interface between MarkOrbit and every consumer.

Every consumer receives Guide Products.

Not internal implementation.

---

# Scope

Guide Products include every published professional experience.

Examples include:

- Trademark Filing Experience
- Renewal Experience
- Portfolio Review Experience
- Office Action Experience
- Country Recommendation Experience
- Brand Protection Experience
- Customer Onboarding Experience

Guide Products are channel-independent.

---

# Responsibilities

Guide Product owns:

- published experience
- interaction summary
- navigation state
- explanation
- user guidance
- experience contract

Guide Product does not own:

- execution
- reasoning
- workflow
- runtime state

---

# Architecture

Every Guide Product follows one architecture.

```text
Capability Product

↓

Guide Objects

↓

Interaction Models

↓

Experience Network

↓

Guide Product
```

Guide Product becomes the public interface of the Professional Operating System.

- Version

- Next Actions

# Validation

Validation should verify:

- completeness
- usability
- accessibility
- explainability
- consistency

Only validated Guide Products should be published.

---

# Runtime

Runtime renders Guide Product Instances.

Definitions remain immutable.

Presentation may vary by channel.

Professional semantics remain unchanged.

---

# Relationship to Workflow

Workflow produces work.

Guide Products communicate completed value.

Workflow remains operational.

Guide Products remain experiential.

---

# Relationship to Workspace

Workspace customizes presentation.

Core experience contracts remain reusable.

---

# Compatibility

Guide Products evolve through versioning.

Breaking experience semantics require:

- new Product Version
- updated Contract
- migration guidance

---

# Extension

Future Guide Products may include:

- Voice Experience Packages
- AR Experience Packages
- Autonomous Agent Packages
- Multi-user Collaboration Packages

Extensions should preserve experience contracts.

---

# Non Goals

Guide Product is not:

- Web Page
- HTML
- Chat History
- React Component
- Mobile Screen

These may render Guide Products.

They are not Guide Products.

# Summary

Guide Product defines the published professional experiences of the MarkOrbit Operating System.

It transforms professional services into stable, explainable and reusable experience interfaces.

Guide Products provide the final value delivered by MarkOrbit, enabling humans, AI agents, external systems and automated workflows to consume professional expertise through consistent experience contracts independent of presentation technology.
