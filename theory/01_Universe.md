# Purpose

This document defines the Trademark Universe.

The Trademark Universe is the reality that exists independently of MarkOrbit.

MarkOrbit does not create this universe.

It observes it.

Models it.

Understands it.

Operates within it.

Everything inside MarkOrbit ultimately corresponds to something within the Trademark Universe.

---

# What Is the Trademark Universe

The Trademark Universe is the complete ecosystem surrounding trademarks, brands and trademark-related activities.

It includes:

- people
- organizations
- legal rights
- commercial assets
- events
- relationships
- jurisdictions
- business activities

The Trademark Universe exists regardless of whether MarkOrbit exists.

---

# Reality First

Reality always comes before software.

```
Trademark Universe

↓

MarkOrbit

↓

Products

↓

Users
```

MarkOrbit never defines reality.

Reality defines MarkOrbit.

---

# The Structure of the Trademark Universe

The Trademark Universe consists of five fundamental domains.

```
Actors

Assets

Events

Relationships

Ecosystem
```

Together they describe every observable object within the trademark industry.

---

# Actors

Actors participate in the Trademark Universe.

Actors make decisions.

Actors perform actions.

Examples include:

- Applicant
- Owner
- Customer
- Agency
- Attorney
- Examiner
- Trademark Office
- Enterprise

Actors may own assets.

Actors may participate in events.

Actors may establish relationships.

---

# Assets

Assets possess legal or commercial value.

Examples include:

- Trademark
- Brand
- Trademark Portfolio
- Domain Name
- Certificate
- License

Assets may evolve.

Assets may be transferred.

Assets may be protected.

Assets may generate business value.

---

# Events

Events represent changes.

Examples include:

- Filing
- Publication
- Registration
- Refusal
- Opposition
- Assignment
- Renewal
- Cancellation

Events produce history.

History produces timelines.

---

# Relationships

Nothing exists in isolation.

Relationships connect the Trademark Universe.

Examples include:

Owner owns Trademark.

Trademark belongs to Brand.

Agency represents Owner.

Attorney handles Case.

Customer manages Portfolio.

Relationships have meaning independent of individual objects.

Relationships may evolve over time.

---

# Ecosystem

The Trademark Universe extends beyond legal rights.

It also includes the environment in which trademarks exist.

Examples include:

- Laws
- Regulations
- Examination Guidelines
- Courts
- Marketplaces
- Registries
- Industries
- Geographic Regions

The ecosystem continuously changes.

MarkOrbit continuously adapts.

---

# Brand and Trademark

Brand and Trademark are different concepts.

A Trademark is a legal right.

A Brand is a commercial identity.

One Brand may contain many Trademarks.

One Trademark may protect only part of a Brand.

MarkOrbit models both independently while maintaining their relationships.

---

# Customer and Owner

Customer and Owner are different concepts.

Owner represents legal ownership.

Customer represents a commercial relationship.

One Customer may manage multiple Owners.

One Owner may be served by multiple organizations over time.

---

# Reality Is Independent

Reality exists regardless of software.

Reality may be:

- incomplete
- inconsistent
- delayed
- uncertain

MarkOrbit should represent reality faithfully.

It should never simplify reality merely to fit implementation.

# Evolution

The Trademark Universe continuously evolves.

New jurisdictions appear.

Laws change.

Business models evolve.

Artificial Intelligence changes professional practice.

MarkOrbit should evolve together with the Trademark Universe without changing its fundamental principles.

---

# Scope

The Trademark Universe includes every object required to understand and operate trademark-related activities.

MarkOrbit may continue expanding this scope over time.

Examples include:

- Brand Management
- Portfolio Management
- Trademark Marketplace
- Licensing
- IP Services
- Cross-border Brand Protection

Expansion should extend the Universe.

It should not redefine it.

---

# Design Principles

Reality before abstraction.

Relationships before hierarchy.

Evolution before stability.

Observation before implementation.

Commercial value and legal value should coexist.

---

# Summary

The Trademark Universe is the reality represented by MarkOrbit.

It consists of actors, assets, events, relationships and the surrounding ecosystem.

Reality exists independently of software.

MarkOrbit exists to observe, model and operate within that reality.

Everything built upon MarkOrbit ultimately begins with the Trademark Universe.
