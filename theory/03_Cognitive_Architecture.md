# Purpose

Reality alone is not sufficient.

Professional work requires understanding.

The purpose of MarkOrbit's Cognitive Architecture is to transform observations into professional cognition.

It explains how facts become knowledge, how knowledge becomes judgment, and how judgment becomes professional capability.

---

# Cognitive Philosophy

Reality exists.

Reality is observed.

Observation becomes facts.

Facts become knowledge.

Knowledge supports judgment.

Judgment enables professional capability.

Professional capability enables execution.

This continuous transformation forms the cognitive foundation of MarkOrbit.

---

# The Cognitive Pipeline

MarkOrbit continuously transforms reality through one cognitive pipeline.

```
Reality

↓

Facts

↓

Knowledge

↓

Judgment

↓

Capability

↓

Execution

↓

Feedback
```

Every layer enriches the previous one.

No layer replaces the previous one.

---

# Reality

Reality is the Trademark Universe.

Reality includes everything that exists independently of MarkOrbit.

Reality cannot be changed by software.

Reality can only be observed.

---

# Facts

Facts describe reality.

Facts answer:

> What happened?

Facts are objective.

Examples include:

- a trademark application was filed
- an owner changed
- a registration expired
- a renewal deadline exists

Facts belong to Data.

---

# Knowledge

Knowledge explains facts.

Knowledge answers:

> What does this mean?

Knowledge is built from:

- laws
- regulations
- examination practice
- professional experience
- business knowledge

Knowledge belongs to Brain.

Knowledge may evolve without changing facts.

---

# Judgment

Judgment applies knowledge to a specific situation.

Judgment answers:

> What should happen next?

Examples include:

- recommend filing
- recommend renewal
- estimate refusal risk
- identify commercial opportunity

Judgment belongs to Intelligence.

Different judgments may exist for the same facts.

---

# Capability

Capability transforms judgment into professional action.

Capability answers:

> How should this be done?

Capability is reusable.

Capability is testable.

Capability is composable.

Capability belongs to Capability Catalog.

---

# Execution

Execution performs business work.

Execution answers:

> Has the work been completed?

Execution belongs to Workflow.

Execution changes reality through business activities.

Execution generates Events.

Events update Timelines.

---

# Feedback

Every execution produces feedback.

Feedback answers:

> What did we learn?

Feedback improves:

- Knowledge
- Intelligence
- Capability

Feedback never rewrites reality.

It improves future cognition.

---

# Cognitive Responsibilities

Each cognitive layer owns one responsibility.

| Layer | Responsibility |
|--------|----------------|
| Reality | Exists |
| Facts | Describe |
| Knowledge | Explain |
| Judgment | Recommend |
| Capability | Execute Professionally |
| Workflow | Execute Business |
| Feedback | Improve |

No responsibility should overlap.

---

# Continuous Learning

MarkOrbit continuously improves through feedback.

```
Reality

↓

Facts

↓

Knowledge

↓

Judgment

↓

Capability

↓

Execution

↓

Feedback

↓

Knowledge
```

Learning never replaces history.

Learning enriches future understanding.

---

# Human and Artificial Intelligence

Professional cognition is not limited to artificial intelligence.

Knowledge may originate from:

- professionals
- organizations
- regulations
- historical practice
- artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence accelerates cognition.

It does not define cognition.

---

# Separation of Cognition

Facts should never become opinions.

Knowledge should never become execution.

Judgment should never become reality.

Execution should never rewrite history.

Every cognitive layer preserves its own responsibility.

---

# Design Principles

Reality before facts.

Facts before knowledge.

Knowledge before judgment.

Judgment before capability.

Capability before execution.

Execution before feedback.

Feedback before improvement.

---

# Summary

MarkOrbit understands the Trademark Universe through a continuous cognitive process.

Reality becomes facts.

Facts become knowledge.

Knowledge supports judgment.

Judgment becomes reusable capability.

Capability enables execution.

Execution produces feedback.

Feedback continuously improves future cognition.

This cognitive architecture enables MarkOrbit to evolve together with the Trademark Universe while preserving the integrity of reality.
